Our Story

QPAY was founded in 2012 by a fintech expert and passionate entreprenuer who spent years working with the SME sector, seeing its struggle to generate profit and growth. Hence, the founder teamed up with highly qualified professionals who believed that together they had a chance to offer something that had not yet been done in Qatar. Giving easy access to financial services to under serviced owners of small businesses. QPAY was formed andf combined exceptional customer technology to turn innovative ideas into real products and services that empower the SMEs and revolutionize the Fintech scene in Qatar. Since then, QPay have been enriching the SME community in Qatar with unmatched financial solutions that have lead to winning multiple awards, gaining the community's trust, and becoming more and more commited to helping owners of small businesses.

We are an industry-leading company that values honesty, integrity, and efficiency. Building quality products and caring for the users are what made us stand out since the beginning.
We’re a team of highly skilled professionals with years of experience in different fields vowed to revolutionize the people’s web experience.
